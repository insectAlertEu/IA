Insect Alert App

Web app informing the public about areas with dangerous insects, snakes, or plants. Project for European Commision for MYGEOSS (digitalearthlab.jrc.ec.europa.eu/mygeoss). The app is published under the European Union Public License (EUPL) Version 1.1.

Contact and Links

App Website: http://www.insect-alert.eu/

MyGEOSS: http://digitalearthlab.jrc.ec.europa.eu/mygeoss/

Funding

This application has been developed within the MyGEOSS project, which has received funding from the European Union's Horizon 2020 research and innovation programme.
