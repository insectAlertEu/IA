How to navigate through this webpage?

The "Project" page is your starting point for browsing Insect Alert site. Open a new page by clicking the name of it. You can choose from Project, Knowledge Base, How to use, About, Status.

Project webpage is the main page, where you can obtain all of interesting information.

Knowledge Base webpage is a base created by our guest, where they may add own data points.

How to use is this page with tutorial.

About webpage contains information of us and MyGEOSS program.

Status page tells about changes and new datasets provided for the website.

Main Page

First of all, you need to decide which Danger Index you want to see. There are four choices: Ticks, Mosiquites, Plants and Snakes. Everytime you click on small black square, more options will show up. You can choose view of the countries in high or low resolutions.

When you choose one of option you can click "read more" and see webpages about selected data and diseases spreading by selected vectors. There are also information about dangerous plants and animals which are not connected to the vector-borne diseases. If you click "Proceed" button the map with will show up. See the image below:



You can hover on region and then information about spreading of vector will appear in right-side window.



For some countries (like Poland or Czech Republic) after left-clicking on region you will see more detailed map with higher spatial resolution.

After right-click the on region, it's possible to add your observations. You can write only your name and information of when you were in contact with vector. Information from users is avaible on the right window from main page (see image above).

Knowledge base

You can access Knowledge Base from "Read more..." link during danger index choosing or through clicking on Knowledge Base is about:
a) Information about dangerous species;
b) Information about vector-borne diseases and other threats for people;
c) Information about data (metadata);
d) Access to raw data, which you can use for your projects.

We highly recommended to check Knowledge Base. Spatial data without medical information is incomplete.
